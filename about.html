What is PresSure

PresSure is an affordable at home breast wellness tool designed for women, especially those 30+ or with elevated personal or family history, who want more clarity between traditional self exams and infrequent clinical imaging.

How It Works

Using a handheld sensor with a force sensing resistor (FSR) and an air pressure bladder, PresSure detects variations in breast tissue stiffness by measuring pressure patterns across quadrants. When paired with a simple and intuitive interface that generates a visual heatmap, the device highlights areas that may warrant earlier clinical attention.

Why PresSure Matters

PresSure is noninvasive, low cost, and user friendly. It fills a critical gap between subjective self exams and expensive imaging by offering an objective and supportive way for women to stay in tune with their breast health at home. It contributes to the growing field of womens health innovation and accessible early awareness tools.
